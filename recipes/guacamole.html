Guacamole

Description

Need something to go along with those delicious chips? Try this flavorful and simple guacamole

Ingredients

  1. Avocados (hopefully they're just right)
  2. A small proportion of diced white onion
  3. chopped Tomatoes (not too much! your not making salsa here
  4. Coarse salt
  5. Lime
  6. Finely diced cilantro

Steps

  1. Mash peeled avocados in a bowl with cilantro, onions and tomatoes
  2. Squeeze a bit of lime on after you've reached your desired consistency
  3. sprinkle with salt and enjoy!

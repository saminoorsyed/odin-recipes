Sopa AZTECA

Description

This delicious shredded chicken soup packs a powerful savory and zesty flavor with its charred ingredients, spices, herbs and freshly fried tortilla chips!

Ingredients

  1. Dried chiles (play around with it! pick a chile that makes your mouth water)
  2. Tomatoes
  3. Onion and garlic
  4. Stock (I usually use chicken stock, but a veggie broth might go well here too)
  5. Dried oregano
  6. Salt and pepper
  7. Cilantro!
  8. Tortilla strips (I like to make my own from corn tortillas)
  9. Avocado (topping)
  10. Chicken(topping)
  11. Cheese(topping)
  12. Jalapenos (optional topping if you like a little kick!)

Steps

  1. toast the chiles and tomatoes, don't be afraid to give them a little char!
  2. Saute the onion and garlic (don't dice that garlic!)
  3. Blend it all together with the broth on a medium heat
  4. Simmer while preparing addons
  5. Season the broth to your liking
  6. Ladel the broth into bowels and add a healthy bit of toppings
